Fullstack shopping website

This e-commerce platform, built with PHP and SQL Workbench, offers comprehensive features. Admins can easily add products with details like names, descriptions, creators, and pricing. They can also create product categories efficiently. The system ensures quick visibility into customer inquiries for prompt responses. Advanced filtering prioritizes unanswered queries. Visitors enjoy intuitive navigation, with a user-friendly dropdown menu for browsing by category. They can pose inquiries directly on product pages. The homepage highlights the latest additions to the product catalog.
