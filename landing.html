Interactive primary school app

This application is specifically designed for primary school use, with a primary focus on user-friendliness and simplicity. The home screen serves as the central hub for a range of educational features, including a notes page, a quiz platform, and a calculator tool. Additionally, a logout button ensures convenient navigation for users.

The calculator page seamlessly integrates into the user experience, providing students with a fully functional tool for mathematical tasks. The notes page empowers users to effortlessly create, organize, and manage their notes, enhancing accessibility and usability. The quiz page offers a dynamic learning experience with approximately five multiple-choice questions, designed to engage and challenge students. Furthermore, the addition of a zoo background and a web view of BBC bitesize adds an enriching visual and informational dimension to the application.
